About Me


Hi, I'm Nilesh Ahirwar (Nick), a passionate Computer Science Engineer dedicated to crafting exceptional digital experiences. My educational journey, shaped by my father's army postings, took me across various cities, culminating in my Higher Secondary completion from Sankatmochan Higher Secondary School in Chhindwara. This diverse exposure instilled in me a deep appreciation for adaptability and continuous learning.
Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I specialize in developing intuitive UI designs and am actively enhancing my backend development skills. My portfolio includes multiple projects, and I currently engage in freelancing, delivering tailored solutions to clients.
My commitment to growth is evident through certifications in Informatics and Computer Basics, Hardware Networking & Troubleshooting, and Cybersecurity. I'm also pursuing a comprehensive three-year diploma in Computer Science and Engineering to solidify my technical foundation.
Beyond coding, I possess a creative flair, with part-time experience in graphic design, photo editing, and form creation. My proficiency in MS Office tools like Word and Excel complements my technical skills, enabling me to handle diverse tasks efficiently.
Driven by a passion for technology and innovation, I continuously seek opportunities to expand my knowledge and contribute meaningfully to the tech community. Let's connect and explore how we can collaborate to bring impactful digital solutions to life.
